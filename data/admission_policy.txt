# University Admission Policy

## Eligibility Criteria
1. Minimum 75% marks in 12th standard (or equivalent)
2. Acceptable streams: Science, Commerce, Arts
3. Age limit: 17-25 years
4. Valid ID proof and address verification

## Required Documents
1. Identity Proof (Aadhar Card/Passport/Driving License)
2. 12th Standard Marksheet
3. Passport size photograph
4. Address Proof
5. Character Certificate from previous institution

## Application Process
1. Fill the online application form
2. Upload all required documents
3. Pay application fee
4. Await document verification
5. Check shortlisting status
6. If shortlisted, pay admission fee or apply for loan
7. Receive admission letter

## Important Dates
- Application Opens: January 15, 2025
- Application Closes: March 15, 2025
- Document Verification: March 15-20, 2025
- Shortlisting Results: March 25, 2025
- Fee Payment Deadline: April 5, 2025
- Classes Begin: April 15, 2025
